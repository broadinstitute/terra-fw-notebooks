# terra-fw-notebooks
A repo for Terra Featured Workspace Notebooks owned and maintained by DSP Customer Delivery

Last update: August 24, 2021

Below are listed the notebooks contained in each of Terra's featured workspaces (FW). The list is organized by workspace, and several notebooks appear in multiple workspaces. Each workspace is listed in the format of "billing project/workspace name", as it appears at the top of the page when inside the workspace, and each entry in the list includes the address of the workspace Google bucket, and a list of Terra tags, if that workspace has any.


The list is subdivided into three categories, based on the organization of the FW page prior to its revamp in August 2021. The three categories were: 

1) "New and interesting" - These are workspaces with new and relevant use cases. Some are there as hubs for data related to a paticular topic, and others are meant to showcase tools and workflows develped for specific projects. As of 2021, the majority of these workspaces were COVID-related.

2) "Tutorials" - The tutorial is the largest one, containing workspaces meant to give hands-on training for various tools, workflows, and Terra features.

3) "GATK best practices" - These workspaces contain GATK workflows preconfigured to the settings specified in their "best practices" documentation, found here: https://gatk.broadinstitute.org/hc/en-us/sections/360007226651-Best-Practices-Workflows

More detail about the contents and organization of the FWs can be found in this article in the Terra knowledge center: https://support.terra.bio/hc/en-us/articles/360028967111
